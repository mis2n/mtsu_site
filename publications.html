PUBLICATIONS

My Research is focused on the development of image datasets for machine learning through crowdsourcing innitiatives such as the Ancient Lives Project. The challen ges involved in such endeavors lead to fundamental questions regarding ground truth in labeling and understanding the behavoirs of volunteer annotators. While many deep learning architecturs have reached super-human levels of accurac y when trained with hundreds of millions of images, improving the data such models are trained on will allow for similarly impressive results with small to medium sized datasets.
